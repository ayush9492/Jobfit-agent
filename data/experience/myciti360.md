# MyCiti 360 Technology Services — Machine Learning Engineer & Data Analyst

**Role:** Machine Learning Engineer and Data Analyst
**Location:** Surat, GJ, India
**Dates:** Jan 2024 – Jul 2024
**Domain:** Computer vision / smart-city analytics

**What I did:**
Implemented ANPR (automatic number-plate recognition) and person-detection pipelines
using TensorFlow and OpenCV, integrating a Python-based unique-ID tracking system
across three site locations. Constructed a real-time analytics dashboard with Flask
and Power BI that ingested over 10,000 vehicle telemetry records per day from MySQL.

**Tools & tech:** TensorFlow, OpenCV, Python, Flask, Power BI, MySQL, ANPR,
person detection, unique-ID tracking

**Metrics / impact:**
- Boosted identification accuracy by ~25% across three site locations.
- Cut incident response time by ~30% with the real-time dashboard.
- Handled >10,000 vehicle telemetry records per day.
